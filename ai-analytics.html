Regulatory Compliance

FEOC Compliance for American BESS

The Inflation Reduction Act's Foreign Entity of Concern (FEOC) requirements demand rigorous supply chain documentation. Our AI Analytics platform automates FEOC compliance so your battery systems qualify for full IRA tax credits.

What is FEOC?

Under the IRA, battery components sourced from or manufactured by Foreign Entities of Concern disqualify BESS projects from critical tax credits. Starting 2024, critical minerals from FEOC entities are excluded; from 2025, battery components from FEOC entities are excluded. American BMS ensures your entire supply chain meets these requirements.
